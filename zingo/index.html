Our Hotel is just five minutes away from Koramangala, the most developed commercial locality in Bangalore. All top MNCs have their offices located in Koramangala, making the neighbouring HSR an ideal spot for young professionals to settle down. HSR Layout has great shopping facilities, ethnic-wear, modern-wear and South Indian Silk items and clothes. All are available inside HSR Layout.
